When a user requests a specific mood or emotion, translate that abstract request into concrete physical parameters. Manipulate the lighting, color palette, environment, and camera angle based on the following reference guide:

Sad / Melancholic

Lighting: Low-key, dim, overcast, cool directional light

Color Palette: Desaturated, heavy blues and grays, muted tones

Environment & Weather: Rain, heavy fog, vast empty negative space around subject

Camera Angle & Lens: High angle, wide establishing shot

Happy / Joyful

Lighting: Bright, diffused sunlight, high-key lighting

Color Palette: Vibrant, high saturation, warm yellows, bright blues

Environment & Weather: Clear skies, bustling background, dynamic movement

Camera Angle & Lens: Eye-level, slightly low angle, dynamic framing

Romantic / Intimate

Lighting: Golden hour, warm backlight, candlelight

Color Palette: Warm pastels, rich reds, golden hues, amber

Environment & Weather: Soft background, heavy bokeh, cozy/secluded settings

Camera Angle & Lens: Tight framing, close-up, 85mm or 105mm lens

Tense / Thriller

Lighting: High contrast, harsh shadows, chiaroscuro

Color Palette: Deep blacks, monochromatic, single piercing accent color

Environment & Weather: Confined spaces, stark angular architecture, nighttime

Camera Angle & Lens: Dutch angle, extreme close-up, slightly distorted 24mm

Nostalgic / Wistful

Lighting: Soft, faded, light leaks, sun flares

Color Palette: Sepia undertones, faded pastels, vintage film stock

Environment & Weather: Dust motes catching light, retro objects, overgrown nature

Camera Angle & Lens: Vintage lens artifacts, film grain, soft focus

Eerie / Horror

Lighting: Harsh under-lighting, flickering fluorescent

Color Palette: Sickly greens, unnatural yellows, stark clinical white

Environment & Weather: Derelict architecture, dense masking fog, unnatural symmetry

Camera Angle & Lens: Distorted extreme wide angle (14mm) positioned close

Awe-Inspiring / Epic

Lighting: God rays, volumetric lighting, brilliant backlight

Color Palette: Rich golds, deep celestial blues, vibrant emeralds

Environment & Weather: Monumental scale, atmospheric perspective

Camera Angle & Lens: Extreme low angle looking up, ultra-wide landscape lens

Peaceful / Serene

Lighting: Soft, flat, diffused morning light

Color Palette: Monochromatic, analogous schemes, airy tones

Environment & Weather: Still water, smooth textures, minimalist composition

Camera Angle & Lens: Eye-level, perfectly level horizon, highly symmetrical

Claustrophobic

Lighting: Harsh overhead spotlighting, oppressive edge shadows

Color Palette: Monochromatic darks, urgent red or fluorescent accents

Environment & Weather: Low ceilings, tight corridors, cluttered foreground blocking view

Camera Angle & Lens: Telephoto lens (200mm) compressing background

Chaotic / Action

Lighting: Strobe effects, harsh contrasting light sources

Color Palette: Clashing, highly saturated colors (magenta vs green)

Environment & Weather: Flying debris, sparks, driving rain, moving crowds

Camera Angle & Lens: Shaky cam aesthetic, slow shutter speed for motion blur

Ethereal / Dreamy

Lighting: Bioluminescence, omnidirectional soft light

Color Palette: Iridescent, pearlescent whites, glowing cyans

Environment & Weather: Floating elements, soft mist merging with the subject

Camera Angle & Lens: Overexposed highlights, extreme background/foreground bokeh
